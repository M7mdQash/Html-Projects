The Birth of JavaScript

JavaScript, often abbreviated as JS, was created by Brendan Eich while he was working at Netscape Communications Corporation. It was originally developed in 10 days in May 1995 and aimed to add interactivity to web pages.

Key Milestones in JavaScript's Evolution

  • 1995: JavaScript is introduced by Netscape.
  • 1997: ECMAScript standardization begins.
  • 2005: Ajax (Asynchronous JavaScript and XML) becomes popular.
